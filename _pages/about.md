I am a Assistant Professor at [Sports Science Laboratory at Komaba](https://idaten.c.u-tokyo.ac.jp/), Graduate School of Arts and Sciences, [The University of Tokyo](https://www.u-tokyo.ac.jp/en/index.html).
I'm interested in how our brains connect music and movements, extending to perception and action dynamics. I am addressing this question with methods combining motion capture system and neuroimaging techniques. I am currently working on inter-brain synchrony related to sharing prior and action intentions during imitative interaction, and the functional and structural connectivities correlate with sensorimotor synchronization skill.
